Press '1' for chase camera, '2' for top camera.
Movement controls:
  • W/S: Translate Forward/Backward
  • A/D: Rotate Left/Right
  • Q/E: Translate Left/Right
  • R/F: Rotate Up/Down
  • Z: Reset position and rotation.
